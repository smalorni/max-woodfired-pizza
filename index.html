Our Story

In the heart of the city, Max's Pizza was born from a single vision: to create the most authentic and mouthwatering pies, reminiscent of the old-world charm of Italian pizzerias. Founded in 1998 by Max Ricci, a third-generation pizza maestro, our eatery proudly kneads tradition into every crust, sources the freshest local ingredients, and fires up each pizza in a classic wood-burning oven. Our dedication to the craft has not only given life to flavors that transport you straight to Naples, but has also cultivated a community where families and friends gather to celebrate life's simplest yet most delectable moments. Welcome to Max's, where every slice tells a story.

Pizzas

At Max's Pizza, every pie is a symphony of flavors, crafted meticulously from age-old family recipes and baked to perfection in a traditional wood-burning oven. Each slice boasts a harmonious blend of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, cradled atop a crispy yet tender crust that's rich in history and taste. Dive into a Max's masterpiece, and experience a delightful journey from the heart of Naples to your very plate.
